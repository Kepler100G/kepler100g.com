Kepler-100G

The Kepler-100G Community

A collaborative worldbuilding project by dedicated creators worldwide

Kepler-100G is built by a diverse community of worldbuilders, writers, artists, designers, and developers from around the globe. Founded with a vision to create an inclusive and accessible creative space, Kepler-100G thrives through the contributions and collaboration of its members. Whether you're crafting stories in ToadVille Bay, designing innovations for Progressia, or celebrating culture in the Union of Blueland, you're part of a shared creative universe. This project combines passion for worldbuilding with a commitment to making collaborative creativity accessible and welcoming to all.

What is Kepler-100G

Kepler-100G is a collaborative worldbuilding project and fictional universe. It features three main nations—ToadVille Bay, Progressia, and the Union of Blueland—each with their own rich histories, cultures, and characters. This community project invites creators to contribute stories, characters, and ideas to the expanding Kepler-100G universe.

About This Project

Kepler-100G.com serves as the central hub for the community. This website showcases the worldbuilding efforts of our members and provides resources for those interested in participating. Whether you're a writer, artist, or simply a fan of collaborative creativity, you'll find opportunities to contribute and connect with the community.

Mission & Vision

The Kepler-100G community is dedicated to creative collaboration, inclusive worldbuilding, and celebrating diverse perspectives. We believe in the power of collective storytelling and aim to create a welcoming space where all members can share their creativity, grow as creators, and help shape a shared fictional universe.
