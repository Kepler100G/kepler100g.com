Kepler-100G Community Rules

Guidelines for respectful, inclusive, and constructive participation in the Kepler-100G community.

Welcome to Kepler-100G

The Kepler-100G community is a roleplay and worldbuilding space where creativity flourishes. These rules ensure everyone has fun, plays fairly, and respects one another. Read them carefully and ask moderators if you have questions!

Core Community Rules

1 No NSFW or R34

Explicit sexual content and R34 material are not permitted. Shipping content is allowed but keep it appropriate and don't go too far.

2 Swearing Guidelines

Swearing is allowed in general conversation. However, do not use profanity to directly attack or harass members Out-Of-Character (OOC). Keep language respectful when addressing other players.

3 Fair Roleplay

Be fair when roleplaying. Do not use god mode or overpowered abilities without reason. Ensure all players have a chance to participate and that challenges are reasonable.

4 No Nuking or Raiding

Do not nuke, raid, or sabotage the server. Destructive behavior that ruins the experience for others is strictly prohibited.

5 Use Appropriate Channels

Use the channels that are intended for specific purposes. Keep roleplay in roleplay channels, discussions in discussion channels, and off-topic content in designated spaces.

6 No OOC Knowledge Abuse

Do not use Out-Of-Character knowledge to gain an advantage in roleplay. Keep IC and OOC information separate and roleplay fairly based only on what your character knows.

7 Respect Everyone

Treat all community members with respect. Respect their characters, their roleplay choices, and their boundaries. A healthy community is built on mutual respect.

8 Use Common Sense

Use common sense in all interactions. Especially during conflicts, wars, or complex scenarios—think before you act. Understand the consequences of your character's actions and roleplay realistically.

Consequences for Rule Violations

Breaking the rules affects everyone's fun. Consequences vary based on severity:

  • Verbal Warning: For minor first-time violations
  • Formal Warning: For repeated or moderate violations
  • Temporary Mute/Timeout: Temporary restrictions on posting or roleplay
  • Suspension: Temporary removal from the server
  • Permanent Ban: Removal for serious, repeated, or intentional violations

Moderators use their judgment to determine appropriate responses based on the situation and context.

Appeals Process

If you believe a moderation decision was made in error, you may submit an appeal through official channels. Appeals should provide relevant context and be submitted within 30 days of the action. Appeals will be reviewed by a moderator team to ensure fairness.

Reporting Violations

If you witness a violation of these rules, please report it to the moderation team immediately. Provide as much detail as possible, including:

  • Description of the violation
  • Links to or screenshots of relevant content
  • Time and date when it occurred
  • Any other relevant context

All reports are taken seriously and treated confidentially.

Final Note

We appreciate your commitment to keeping Kepler-100G fun and fair for everyone. If you see someone breaking rules, report it to moderators. If you have questions about the rules, ask! We're here to help you have an awesome time. Thanks for being part of the community!
