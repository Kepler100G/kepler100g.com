Frequently Asked Questions

Is Kepler-100G free to join?

Yes! Kepler-100G is completely free to join. Our Discord community is open to anyone interested in worldbuilding and collaboration.

Do I need experience to join?

No experience necessary! Whether you're a seasoned worldbuilder or completely new, you're welcome here. Our community is beginner-friendly and supportive.

Can I contribute my own creations?

Absolutely! We encourage members to contribute original characters, locations, stories, and ideas to the Kepler-100G universe.

What if I have questions?

Join our Discord and ask away! Our community members and moderators are happy to help answer questions and guide you.

Is there a character limit on how many creations I can contribute?

No limit! Contribute as much as you'd like. We celebrate active and engaged community members.

What are the community guidelines?

We maintain a respectful, inclusive community. Check out our full community rules for details.

How often are events held?

We host regular events, writing prompts, discussions, and community activities. Details are shared in Discord.

Ready to Join?

The first step is simple: join our Discord server. Once you're in, introduce yourself, explore the channels, and start connecting with community members. We're excited to have you as part of Kepler-100G!
